OpenEduCat LMS

Online Demo

This module adds the feature of learning management system to OpenEduCat. You can create online course and publish it.

Contact Us

Course

Create your online course, add materials to it and publish it. Invite users to join the course.

Visibility Policy

Set visibility to everyone, logged in users only and invited users only of course.

Navigation Policy

Create saperate blog and forum for the particular course. Create blog post and forum post to discuss about that course.

Description

Add your short and full description of course to display on the page. Show what your course created for and what content user will find and learn.

Material

Create material for course and publish it online. Set your publish policy for your material when you want to publish.

Material Types

Video

Create video as material, You can set Youtube video as your material and display it.

Image

Create material of image and display your information as picture, you can display graph or anything you want as image.

Document

Show your data or content as document that you have prepared. User can navigate through whole document.

Enrollment

Get details of enrollment for the users who enrolled for the course. Course starting date, completion date and material viewed.

Dashboards

Course Home Page

Course home page that display courses which are published online. Navigate by categories, search for the particular course by search.

Course Curriculum

Course curriculum that describes the detail of the course materials. How many section are there in course and materials section wise. Can take preview from that.

Instructor Profile View

Basic introduction of the instructor who created course.

Material View

Display and navigate from all the material which are in course.
